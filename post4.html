Health and Physical Education

In Level 1 Active Wellbeing is compulsory. The classes are:
- L1 Active Wellbeing (1AWB)
- L1 Health Education (1HED)
- L1 Physical Education (1PED)
- L1 Outdoor Education (1OED)
In Level 2 Health and Physical Education is not compulsory. The classes are:
- L2 Early Childhood Education (2ECE)
- L2 Health Education (2HED)
- L2 Physical Education (2PED)
- L2 Sports Studies (2SPS)
- L2 Outdoor Education (2OED)
- L2 Outdoor Ed Alternative (2ODA)
In Level 3 Health and Physical Education is not compulsory. The classes are:
- L3 Early Childhood Education (3ECE)
- L3 Health Education (3HED)
- L3 Physical Education (3PED)
- L3 Sports Studies (3SPS)
- L3 Outdoor Education (3OED)
