Mathematics

In Level 1 Mathematics is compulsory. The classes are:
- L1 Mathematics & Statistics (1MAT)
- L1 Mathematics & Statistics - Alternative (1MAA)
- L1 Mathematics & Statistics - Foundation (1MAF)
- L2 Maths & Stats - Accelerated (2MAX)
In Level 2 Mathematics is not compulsory. The classes are:
- L2 Mathematics & Statistics (2MAT)
- L2 Mathematics & Statistics - Alternative (2MAA)
- L2 Mathematics & Statistics - Foundation (2MAF)
- L2 Maths & Stats - Extended (2MAE)
- L3 Calculus - Accelerated (3MAX)
In Level 2 Mathematics is not compulsory. The classes are:
- L3 Mathematics (3MAT)
- L3 Statistics & Probability (3SAP)
- L3 Statistics & Probability Alternative (3SPA)
- L3 Calculus (3MAC)
