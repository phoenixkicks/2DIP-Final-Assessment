Botany Downs Secondary College Subjects

At Botany Downs Secondary College, the selection of option subjects is important and should be made with personal reflection and open dialogue between students, parents and staff.

Options

Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3

Level 1

English, Mathematics, Science and Active Well Being are compulsory and students can choose THREE other options from a wide range of subjects.

Level 2

Only English is compulsory and students can choose FIVE other options.

Level 3

There are no compulsory subjects and students choose FIVE option subjects.
