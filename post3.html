English

In Level 1 English is compulsory. The classes are:
- L1 English Extension (1ENE)
- L1 English (1ENG)
- L1 English - Alternative (1ENA)
- L1 English - Literacy (1ENL)
In Level 2 English is compulsory. The classes are:
- L2 English Extension (2ENE)
- L2 English (2ENG)
- L2 English - Alternative (2ENA)
In Level 3 English is not compulsory. The classes are:
- L3 English - Extension (3ENE)
- L3 English (3ENG)
- L3 English Booster (3ENB)
