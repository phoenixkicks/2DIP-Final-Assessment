Science

In Level 1 Science is compulsory. The classes are:
- L1 Physics, Earth & Space Science (1SCP)
- L1 Biology/Chemistry (1SCB)
- L1 Science (SCI)
In Level 2 Science is not compulsory. The classes are:
- L2 Physics (2PHY)
- L2 Biology (2BIO)
- L2 Chemistry (2CHE)
In Level 3 Science is not compulsory. The classes are:
- L3 Physics (3PHY)
- L3 Biology (3BIO)
- L3 Chemistry (3CHE)
